# About Me

I believe good infrastructure should be reliable, well documented and built on proven technologies.

My approach is to understand the business requirement first, evaluate the available options and implement practical, maintainable solutions aligned with industry best practices.

I use automation and AI-assisted workflows to improve efficiency, accelerate research and streamline documentation. Every solution is reviewed, tested and validated against business requirements, vendor best practices and real-world operational considerations before implementation.
